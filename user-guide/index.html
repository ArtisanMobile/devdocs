Getting Started With Artisan

Artisan is an all-in-one platform for mobile analytics, A/B testing, personalization, and marketing automation. We help you better understand you app, give you the ability to send push notifications, personalize the content you deliver to your users and much more.

Before You Get Started

Before you get started, you're going to need a few things:

  • An Artisan user account
  • Access to your app's code to install the Artisan SDK

Adding Your App

Adding an app to the Artisan platform is a simple process, but needs to be done by a developer.

  • After logging in to Artisan, click the "Add an App" button located at the top left of the list of apps.
  • Give you app a name and select your OS type (iOS or Android).

Now that you've created an app in Artisan you need to add the Artisan SDK to your app.
