Getting Started With Artisan

Artisan is an all-in-one platform for mobile analytics, A/B testing, personalization, and marketing automation. We help you better understand you app, give you the ability to send push notifications, in-app messages, personalize the content you deliver to your users and much more.

Gaining Access to the Platform

Before you get started, you're going to need access to the Artisan platform and SDK.

  • Purchase a subscription
  • Request a demo

Inviting Team Members

Once you have access to the platform, you're going to need to:

  • Invite team members to the platform (manage users)
  • Email your developers to request the SDK install (click to email)

Adding Your First App

Adding an app to the Artisan platform is a simple process, but needs to be done by a developer with access to your app's source code. This process typically takes about 15 minutes to complete.

  • After logging in to Artisan, click the "Add Your First App" button
  • Give you app a name and select your OS type (iOS or Android)

Now that you've created an app in Artisan you need to add the Artisan SDK to your app.

  • iOS Developer Guide
  • Android Developer Guide
